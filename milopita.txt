dough:
------
125 g margarine (a bit more than a stick)
[ 1 Turkish coffee cup oil (1/4 cup) ]
1 egg
1 teaspoon baking powder
2.5 cups flower
1/2 teaspoon sugar

apple filling:
--------------
1 kg apples -- not too sour
1 cup sugar
2 teaspoons cornflour (at the end, with a bit of water,
to prevent the apple sauce from running)
- dash of nutmeg
- dash of cinnamon
- dash of salt
